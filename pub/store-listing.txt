Full description:
This app adds Union Pearson Express train information to MonTransit.

This app provides the train schedule and news from YouTube and @UPexpress on Twitter.

UP Express train serves Pearson Airport (YYZ), Weston, Bloor and Union stations in the Greater Toronto Area in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by GO Transit.
http://www.gotransit.com/publicroot/en/schedules/DeveloperResources.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-gta-up-express-train-android

This app is not related with GO Transit and Metrolinx.

- Other: required to read news from YouTube and Twitter
